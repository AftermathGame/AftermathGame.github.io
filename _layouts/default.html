Aftermath is an iOS/Android app designed with an emphasis on direct interaction with nearby players and rewarding players for exploration. Aftermath focuses on in-depth character customization that allows players to build their character in a way that matches their preferred playstyles.

Project Description

The game utilizes the GPS functionality of your device as well as it's internet connection in order to make the game world and the real world one and the same. As you cover ground in the real world, your character is scavenging for weapons, equipment, and loot items. All player interaction is proximity based, and whether or not you're spotted is dependent on how you built your character's stats. Players can choose to engage in combat with other players, cooperate with other players for mutual benefit, or attempt to steal from other players if they haven't been spotted. After a day of exploration and interaction, you can return to a location you've marked as your home base in order to be safe from attacks and restore health. The character customization system allows you to choose between numerous clothing and weapon options to suit your preferred playstyle.
